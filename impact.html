SANKETA Project Report: Impact Highlights

Sanketa is an AI-powered adaptive traffic signal system designed for Indian road conditions. The project aims to transform traffic management, reduce congestion, and improve emergency response times across India.

  • 25-50% reduction in average vehicle delay at intersections
  • 40-60% faster emergency vehicle response times
  • 30-45% reduction in fuel consumption at traffic signals
  • 20-35% decrease in carbon emissions
  • Significant improvement in overall traffic flow and road safety

Deployment Strategy

  • Pilot: 10-15 intersections in Bengaluru, Mumbai, or Delhi NCR
  • Expansion: 100-150 intersections in 5-10 tier-1 and tier-2 cities
  • National Rollout: 1000+ intersections in 50+ cities

Applications & Impact

Key outcome areas powered by adaptive, energy-aware signalling and priority logic.

  • Smoother Traffic Flow

    Sanketa’s AI-driven signals adapt green durations in real time, reducing wait and improving travel consistency.

  • Fuel & Emission Savings

    Lower idle times cut fuel use and emissions, supporting cleaner urban air.

  • Reliable Sustainable Operation

    Solar power plus battery backup keeps signals active during outages or weak connectivity periods.

  • Emergency Vehicle Priority

    RFID integration grants rapid green phases for ambulances and responders, accelerating critical journeys.
