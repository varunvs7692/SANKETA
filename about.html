Vision

Sanketa exists to make Indian urban mobility cleaner, safer, and more predictable by turning every traffic signal into a responsive, intelligent edge device.

Approach

  • Edge Intelligence: Decisions made locally for millisecond responsiveness & offline resilience.
  • Energy Independence: Solar power + battery reduces grid strain & ensures uptime.
  • Inclusive Detection: Two-wheelers, buses, pedestrians & emergency vehicles recognized.
  • Transparent Operations: Public countdowns & open APIs reduce stress and risky driving.
  • Sustainability: Lower idle emissions contribute to cleaner air & better health outcomes.

Why Bharat Needs Adaptive Signals

Rapid urbanization, diverse vehicle mix, and infrastructure strain mean fixed-timer signals no longer suffice. Sanketa bridges technology & civic need with affordable sensors and robust software.

Technology Pillars

  • Multi-sensor fusion (camera, radar, loop data)
  • AI classification & queue prediction
  • Priority logic for emergency & high-capacity vehicles
  • Secure edge-to-cloud telemetry (optional)
  • Open SPaT (signal phase & timing) countdown feed
